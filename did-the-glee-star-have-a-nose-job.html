When comparing her photos from the past to the present, there is no discernible difference in her appearance. Leah Michele, on the other hand, has proven them wrong with her natural beauty by not having plastic surgery.

The songwriter is also proud to be on a positive show and a voice for women. She believes that you don't have to look like everyone else to be happy with yourself. This demonstrates her opposition to plastic surgery, and she may have accepted her true self rather than undergoing any procedure.

Lea Michele was always aware of how other people perceived her, which is why, prior to Glee, she never dared to dream of seeing herself on television, on magazine covers, or as the face of beauty campaigns.
