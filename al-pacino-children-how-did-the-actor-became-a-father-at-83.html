Al Pacino children: How did the actor became a father at 83?

July 2024 · 4 minute read

Al Pacino is a legendary actor who has starred in iconic films such as The Godfather, Scarface and Scent of a Woman.

The 83-year-old has never been married, but he has four children with three different women: Julie Marie Pacino, Anton James Pacino, Olivia Pacino and Noor Alfala.

Al Pacino children have followed their father’s footsteps into the entertainment industry, working as filmmakers, actors and producers.

Here are more details about Al Pacino children and their mothers.

Julie Marie Pacino

Julie Marie Pacino, the eldest child and only daughter of Al Pacino, was born on October 16, 1989, to Al Pacino and his ex-girlfriend Jan Tarrant, who is an acting coach.

Growing up between Los Angeles and New York, she had the opportunity to accompany her father on his film sets, sparking her interest in the world of filmmaking.

She pursued her passion for film by earning a degree in film from UCLA and further enhancing her skills at the New York Film Academy.

Today, Julie Marie Pacino is not only a filmmaker but also the founder of two production companies, namely Poverty Row Entertainment and Tiny Apples.

Her directorial, production, and writing credits include an impressive range of short films, documentaries, and web series such as:

  • Billy Bates
  • Abracadabra
  • The Girl Who Couldn’t Sleep

Her exceptional talent as a filmmaker was recognized when she received the Best Female Director award at the 2020 Venice Shorts Film Festival for her remarkable work, Exit 121.

Beyond her contributions to the film industry, Julie Marie Pacino is an active advocate for various causes, including mental health awareness, animal rights, and environmental protection.

She attributes her artistic aspirations and grounded nature to the support and guidance provided by her father throughout her journey.

Al Pacino twins: Anton James Pacino and Olivia Pacino

Anton James Pacino and Olivia Pacino are Al Pacino’s twins with his former girlfriend Beverly D’Angelo, an actress known for her roles in National Lampoon’s Vacation, American History X, and Entourage.

They were born on January 25, 2001, in Los Angeles, California.

They were conceived via in vitro fertilization after Al Pacino expressed his desire to have more children with Beverly D’Angelo.

They have a close bond with their father and often accompany him to red carpet events and award shows.

Anton James Pacino is a budding actor who has appeared in films such as The Wannabe (2015) and Hangman (2017).

He is also a painter who has showcased his artwork on Instagram.

Olivia Pacino is an aspiring producer who has worked on projects such as The Girl Who Couldn’t Sleep (2020) and Find a Way or Make One (2020).

She is also a social media influencer who has over 100k followers on Instagram.

She is currently dating musician Ryan Harley.

Roman Pacino: Al Pacino baby with Noor Alfallah

Al Pacino’s fourth child was born recently.

His name is Roman Pacino and he is his first child with his girlfriend Noor Alfallah, a 29-year-old producer.

The couple have been dating since 2020 and revealed they were expecting a baby in late May.

Roman Pacino joins his three older siblings: Julie Marie Pacino, Anton James Pacino and Olivia Pacino..

Al Pacino has faced some challenges and criticism for becoming a father again at an advanced age.

Al Pacino children: A Source of joy and inspiration

Al Pacino has often spoken about how much he loves his children and how they have changed his life for the better.

In a 2015 interview with The Guardian, he said: “Before I had my three, I’d walk around in my own head, not noticing anything. Acting used to be everything; now, because of them, it’s just a small part.”

He also revealed that his children inspire him to keep working and improving as an actor.

“They make me feel good about what I do,” he told The New Yorker in 2014. “They make me want to do better.”

Conclusion

Al Pacino is one of the most celebrated and respected actors in Hollywood history, but he is also a devoted and proud father of four.

His children have inherited his passion and talent for the entertainment industry, and have also supported him through his highs and lows.

Despite the age gap and the controversy, Al Pacino and Noor Alfallah seem to be happy and in love, and their baby boy Roman Pacino is a testament to their bond.

Al Pacino’s children are his legacy and his inspiration, and he hopes to share his wisdom and experience with them as they grow up.
