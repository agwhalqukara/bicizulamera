Khloe Kardashian, French Montana, No Sex, Observing Ramadan: Video

August 2024 · 2 minute read

TMI, Khloe! Khloe Kardashian crashed boyfriend French Montana‘s Angie Martinez interview for Power 105 on Thursday, July 24 and overshared a little on the state of the couple’s intimate relationship.

Characteristically forthright, the 30-year-old Kardashian refused to bow down to critics who suggest rapper Montana, 29, is no good for her.

“People are going to comment no matter what,” she said. “If I like [Montana], no one else should care, they’re not f–king him. So why do they care?”

In fact, she later admitted, neither is she at the moment. Montana is currently observing Ramadan, an annual month of fasting in the Muslim calendar.

“That’s no eating, no drinking, no smoking,” said show host Martinez. “Wait—there’s no sex! How’s that been?”

“So fun,” Kardashian replied, sarcastically “That’s why he’s wearing all white. He’s so pure and holy, he’s virginal.”

“You could technically torture him right now,” said Martinez, laughing.

“I have been,” Kardashian added cheekily. “Every single day.”

Earlier in the interview the reality TV star revealed Montana’s got a softer side to the one he portrays in the media. “He’s probably one of the funniest people I’ve ever met in my life, you cannot stop laughing, crying laughing,” she said of her beau. “I think he puts out there a tough exterior but there’s another side of him that he keeps more private. I’ve learned from my past you’ve got to keep some things private, a little more close to your heart.”

For Montana’s part, he’s still getting used to the added attention, he claims, insisting he doesn’t really want to do interviews at the moment. “Khloe’s cool as hell,” he said when Martinez pushed him. “Puff is the one who put us together.”

While trying to revert the conversation back to his music, Montana did admit he’ll be starring on the new season of Keeping Up With the Kardashians.
