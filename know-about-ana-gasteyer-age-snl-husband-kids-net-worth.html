Ana Gasteyer is an American actress and comedian best known for her appearance on Saturday Night Live (SNL). Currently, she appears on the family-friendly comedy series, Going There with Ana Gasteyer on Yahoo network.

Ana Gasteyer Bio; Parents, Education, Height

Ana Kristina Gasteyer was born on 4 May 1967 to parents Phil Gasteyer and Mariana Roumell-Gasteyer. Her father is the former mayor of Corrales, New Mexico and her mother is an artist. She grew up with her family in Washington, D.C.

Gasteyer with a height of 5 feet 5.5 inches (1.66 meters) attended Sidwell Friends High School. Afterward, she went to the Northwestern University School of Communication and earned her graduation from there.

Her Career; SNL, Net Worth

Ana joined the cast of Saturday Night Live (SNL) in 1996. Her popular characters were high school music teacher Bobbie Mohan-Culp and National Public Radio Delicious Dish host Margaret Jo McCullen. She appeared on the show until 2002.
