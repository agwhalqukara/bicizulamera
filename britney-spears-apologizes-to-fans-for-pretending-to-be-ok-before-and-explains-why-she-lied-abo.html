Britney Spears Apologizes To Fans For Pretending To Be OK Before And Explains Why She Lied Abo

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Britney Spears assured her fans before that she was ‘ok’ while they expressed concern over her wellbeing and now, she is apologizing for lying to them! Following her first public court appearance regarding her conservatorship battle, the singer took to her Instagram page to admit her life is ‘not perfect at all’ – not for any lack of pretending!

Here’s why Britney Spears previously chose to lie about this!

‘I do not want people to think that my life is perfect because IT IS DEFINITELY NOT AT ALL…and if you’ve read anything about me in the news this week…you obviously know now it is not. I apologize for pretending like I have been ok the past 2 years…I did it [because] of my pride and was embarrassed to share what happened to me,’ Britney wrote in her latest post on her go-to platform.

The pop princess went on to claim that pretending she was doing perfectly fine before actually helped her cope with the conservatorship under her father.

‘Believe it or not pretending that I am ok has actually helped.’
