The Funniest Tweets about Zac Efrons YOLO Tattoo

July 2024 · 3 minute read

In late 2011, rapper, Drake released a song that would prove, once and for all, that only living once is more than enough.  “The Motto,” popularized the acronym YOLO (you only live once) and Drake fans everywhere quickly fell in love with the catchy phrase’s trendy new nickname.  Actor, Zac Efron was among those swept up in the madness.  Not long after the song’s release, the People’s Choice Award winning star appeared in public with “YOLO” tattooed on the side of his right hand.  While Efron proudly sported his new ink, the public quickly responded in disgust with comments like: “Why do some people feel the need to make their skin look like the binder of a seventh grader? At least when you doodle on your binder, you will be getting a new one in September.” While the phrase YOLO has since made its way into irrelevancy, even after all this time, fans are still unhappy with Efron’s decision to permanently etch the initials on to his hand. Here are some five of the funniest tweets about Zac Efron’s “YOLO” tattoo.

5.  @_alyssaaustin  “Just learned that Zac Efron has gotten YOLO tattooed on his hand and I am now crying… Y”

According to this Twitter user, Zac Efron’s tattoo was such a bad decision that it was worthy of tears.

4. @rose_corcoran  “#2013TaughtMe nothing literally i still dont know why math is relevant or why people backstab eachother or why zac efron has a yolo tattoo”

This Twitter user argues that they learned absolutely nothing in 2013. Not being able to understand why Zac Efron thought his “YOLO” tattoo was a good idea is apparently very unsettling.

3. @twelveoclocke “i wish That Awkward Moment was actually a movie where Zac Efron just has to repeatedly explain his yolo tattoo to his grandparents”

Efron’s 2014 film, That Awkward Moment, is a romantic comedy that follows a group of friends who are trying to deal with taking their relationships to the next level.  The film, which received mostly negative reviews, may have been better off taking plot suggestions from Twitter.

2.  @QuitFakinJAX “If u ever felt like you made the biggest mistake of your life, just remember that Zac Efron has a yolo tattoo”

This tweet is the modern day version of the old adage, “somebody else always has it worse.”

1. @natalie_fisherr  “Zac Efron: “give me a YOLO tattoo” Tattoo artist: “are you sure? Tattoos are permanen-” Zac Efron: “yolo”

These tweet reenacts a fictional conversation between Efron and the tattoo artist who permanently branded Efron as a YOLO-er.  While Efron’s tattoo may not be the most meaningful, at least he is living out the phrase’s true meaning.
