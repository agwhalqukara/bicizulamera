Ludacris' Wife Eudoxie Is Pregnant: See Her Baby Announcement

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Now that's a Luda-kiss! Ludacris' wife Eudoxie announced via Instagram on Sunday, March 1, that the two are expecting their first child together.

Eudoxie revealed the news with a sweet photo of her rapper husband, 37, kissing her baby bump in some gorgeous tropical destination. In the sunny snap, the future mom proudly flaunts her burgeoning bump in a turquoise bikini, as she poses with one hand behind her head.

Her husband, meanwhile, leans down with his eyes closed as he pecks her bare bump. "Beautiful day on the beach," Eudoxie captioned the pic, which was reposted minutes later by Ludacris.

The couple's announcement comes just a few months after they got engaged — and hours later, tied the knot last December. In a rather romantic gesture at the time, the Furious 7 actor surprised his fiancee with a spur-of-the-moment wedding, including a stunning white wedding gown.

His rep later confirmed to Us Weekly that the rapper was married over the holidays, and Ludacris directly confirmed the news himself by sharing a photo from his secret nuptials on Instagram.

This is the first child for the newlywed couple, and the third for Ludacris (real name: Christopher Brian Bridges). The star shares two daughters Karma, 14, and Cai Bella, 1.
